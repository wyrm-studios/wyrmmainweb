WYRM — Personal Brand & Studio Identity

Personal brand and creative studio identity system

Brand Identity • Motion Design • Web Design

WYRM — a name inspired by the legendary dragon, symbolizing power, wisdom, and transformation. This is my personal brand and creative studio identity, representing my journey as a motion designer and creative director.

The dragon has always been a symbol that resonates with me — mythical, powerful, and unforgettable. Just like the brands I create for my clients, WYRM embodies strength, creativity, and the ability to leave a lasting impression.

This case study showcases the complete brand identity system I developed for myself, including logo design, visual identity, brand strategy, motion design, and web presence. Every element was carefully crafted to reflect my creative vision and professional approach.
